Blastoids - a simple asteroids clone
Copyright (C) 2005 Rob Loach


DESCRIPTION
	An enhanced version of the classic game: Asteroids.  Control
	your ship, destroying asteroids, gaining points, getting
	powerups and progressing through levels.


FEATURES
	- Classic asteroid gameplay
	- Detailed control system
	- Powerups
	- Highscore list
	- Music and sound
	- Great graphics in high and low detail


GAMEPLAY
	There are two different ways of controlling your ship.  You can
	change control modes in the options menu.  These are the two
	different control modes:
	- Mouse Control
		Use the mouse to control the direction of your ship
		along with the right mouse button to thrust forward
		and the left mouse button to shoot.
	- Keyboard Control
		Use the arrow keys to turn and move your ship and the
		spacebar to shoot.

	Also in the options menu, you'll notice the ability to change
	graphic detail.  It is recommended to use low graphic detail
	if your computer has a slow processing speed.

	You'll find various powerups on your journey.  Gather these to
	assist you:
		Red - Weapon Upgrade
		Green - Thrusters Upgrade
		Blue - Sheild Repair
		Yellow - Bonus Points
		Grey - Extra Life
